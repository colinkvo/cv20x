My 20 Time Project: Building a Website

For my personal 20 Time Project, I decided to revisit something that I haven't done in a while. As a senior I've learned much more than "Hello World!" and this is my showcase of that ability. What is CSIP?

Cyber Security and Information Protection or CSIP is the base for computer and network protection from cyber crime. Cyber crime is one of the fastest growing crimes in the world, and to counter-act it, cyber security is rapidly growing as well. Combined, MCVTS's CSIP Share-Time Program and CCM's Cybersecurity Center of Excellence provides a unique dual-enrollment experience for high school juniors and seniors who want to learn more about the field.



Examples of Cyber Security in Our Everyday Lives:

  • Password Encryption
    • In order to keep our information safe, we need strong passwords, which is, although very simple, a vital part to cyber security.

  • Network Security
    • To prevent malicious attacks on our networks we use everyday, methods of cyber security are used to protect them.

  • Application Security
    • Similar to network security, cyber security methods are applied in applications to ensure that threats cannot take the information attackers are looking for.

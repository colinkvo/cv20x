What Do We Do in CSIP?

As CSIP students, we study a variety of tech-related content, ranging from IT to Programming, all closely related to Cybersecurity. We attend classes at the County College of Morris in the mornings, and return to our high schools for our regularly scheduled classes. Many of us are going into the IT, cyber, or engineering fields, and some of us have completed the IT degree at CCM before high school graduation.



Things We Do:

  • Learn about and practice hands-on with computers in lab activities
  • Learn and write programs in Java, Python, Visual Basic, and more
  • Learn about the Windows, MacOS, Linux, and Unix operating systems and their functionalities
  • Go on field trips to learn about Cybersecurity and related fields
  • Create websites like this one
  • Write scripts in bash for Linux servers
  • Learn about and practice ethical hacking methods

More on what we do on the classes page.
